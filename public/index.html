How do I get the cookies? (~1 minute)

Any browser that has the cookies.txt extension works — Chrome on a computer, or on Android a browser that supports add-ons (Firefox, Kiwi; plain Chrome-for-Android may not). Steps:

  1. Open the browser, go to youtube.com, make sure you’re logged in.
  2. Install “Get cookies.txt LOCALLY” (Chrome Web Store) or “cookies.txt” (Firefox add-ons).
  3. On youtube.com, open the extension → Export (saves a file) or Copy.
  4. Back here: tap “Upload cookies.txt” for the file, or “…or paste cookies” and paste the copied text.
